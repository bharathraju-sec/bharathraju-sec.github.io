// 01 — About

The Person
Behind the Profile

Network engineer, cybersecurity student, problem solver — someone who genuinely enjoys understanding how systems work, and how they break.

I'm Bharath Raju Subramani, a cybersecurity postgraduate student at Macquarie University, Sydney. With over four years of industry experience across broadcast media and enterprise environments in India and Australia, I've built a strong foundation in network infrastructure, enterprise support, and secure system operations.

My most recent role as a Field Network Engineer at ENbridge IT Services in Sydney gave me hands-on exposure to real enterprise infrastructure deployments — installing, configuring, and troubleshooting network devices across client and vendor environments, coordinating ISP activations, and working alongside remote engineering teams.

Before that, three years as a Network Engineer at VNews24 Kannada in Bengaluru built my core technical instincts: routers, switches, firewalls, load balancers, network security measures, performance monitoring, and documentation.

I'm now channelling that deep infrastructure background into cybersecurity — specifically security operations, threat analysis, penetration testing, and building resilient digital environments. The network engineer in me knows exactly what attackers are looking for.
